## Competitor Keywords

### Why Include Competitor Names?

Users searching for "anki" or "quizlet" are looking for flashcard apps. Including these keywords helps LexiconFlow appear in their search results.

**Note**: This is allowed by App Store guidelines as long as we don't misrepresent our app.

### Competitor Keywords Used

- **anki** - Most popular spaced repetition app

## Submission Instructions

### App Store Connect

1. Navigate to **My Apps → LexiconFlow → App Information**
2. Find **Keywords** section
3. Paste keyword string (no spaces after commas)
4. Click **Save**

### Best Practices

- ✅ Use all 100 characters
- ✅ Separate with commas (no spaces)
- ✅ Include high-volume keywords
- ✅ Include niche keywords (spaced repetition, SRS)
- ✅ Include competitor names (anki, quizlet)
- ❌ Don't repeat words
- ❌ Don't use misspellings
- ❌ Don't include brand name (automatic)
- ❌ Don't use plural and singular (flashcard, flashcards)
