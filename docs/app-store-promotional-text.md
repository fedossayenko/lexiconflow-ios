## Submission Instructions

### App Store Connect

1. Navigate to **My Apps → LexiconFlow → App Information**
2. Find **Promotional Text** section
3. Paste chosen promotional text
4. Click **Save**
5. Click **Add for Review** when ready

### Best Practices

- ✅ Use exact character limit (170 characters)
- ✅ Include FSRS v5 mention (differentiator)
- ✅ Highlight 90% retention (social proof)
- ✅ Mention Liquid Glass design (visual appeal)
- ❌ Don't use emojis (not professional)
- ❌ Don't use ALL CAPS (hard to read)
- ❌ Don't include URLs (not allowed)
